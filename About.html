WHO WE ARE

Welcome to the official "About Us" page of the Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka (CAASL). We are the regulatory authority responsible for overseeing and enhancing the safety, security, and sustainability of civil aviation in the beautiful island nation of Sri Lanka. Our mission is to ensure that the skies over Sri Lanka remain safe and accessible to all, while promoting growth and innovation in the aviation industry.

Our Vision

Connecting Skies, Ensuring Safety, Nurturing Excellence.

Who We Are

The Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka is a statutory body established under the Civil Aviation Act No. 34 of 2002. We operate under the guidance of the Ministry of Transport and regularly collaborate with international aviation organizations to maintain the highest standards of aviation safety and security.

Our Commitment

At CAASL, our commitment is to the people of Sri Lanka and all those who travel to and from our shores. We work tirelessly to ensure that aviation in Sri Lanka adheres to global best practices and standards. Safety is our utmost priority, and we leave no stone unturned in our pursuit of making air travel in Sri Lanka secure, efficient, and environmentally responsible.

Our Team

Our dedicated team consists of experienced aviation professionals, regulatory experts, air traffic controllers, and many others who work collaboratively to ensure the highest level of safety and security for aviation in Sri Lanka.
